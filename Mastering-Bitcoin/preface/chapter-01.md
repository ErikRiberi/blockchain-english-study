# Introduction - What Is Bitcoin?

## Summary 

Bitcoin is a collection of concepts an protocols that form a digital money ecosystem.
The Bitcoin protocol stack is available as open source software, which facilitates its accessibility.
In a sense, Bitcoin is the perfect form of money for the internet because you can do many things that can be also done with conventional currencies.
Bitcoin has no physical coins. Ownership is proven through cryptographic keys, which allow users to sign transactions and transfer value to others.
Bitcoin transactions are verified through a mathematical process called mining. In this process, miners use computer power to solve problems and are rewarded with new bitcoin.
Bitcoin tiene un protocolo que regula la funcion de minado, para que independientemente de las condiciones cada 10 minutos, alguien tenga exito. Ademas la velocidad de creeacion de bitcoin se reduce a la mitad cada 4 a;os, habiendo un total presblecido. Produciendo que el suministro de circulacion se aproxime a una curva predecible, sea deflacionario con el paso del tiempo y no haya inflacion devido a que no es posible crear dinero nuevo más allá del ritmo previsto por el protocolo.

Bitcoin has a protocol that regulates the mining function. The creation rate of Bitcoin, is reduced over time. This means that supply of circulation can be follow a predictable curve, and the currency cannot be inflated.
